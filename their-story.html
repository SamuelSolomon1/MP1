OUR STORY

Mai

Mai (ﾒｲﾘｱ), known professionally as Mizuhashi Mai (水橋舞, Mai Mizuhashi), is a singer-songwriter and utaite known for her strong, mature, and clear yet slightly husky voice. She first started singing professionally at the age of 11, and as such, she is a very strong singer technically; possessing a wide vocal range and a firm hold on pitches and tones.




Toku

Toku-P (とくP, also known as beatica) is the professional musician Abe Yoshinori. He made his debut on Niconico in May 2009. Naturally, the completeness of his work is very high, and his first work received more than 50,000 views. He is currently part of the music group outside of VOCALOID known as MARiA, alongside with vocalist Mai .
