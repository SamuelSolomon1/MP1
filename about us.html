MARiA

A unit consisting of female vocalist MAi and composer Toku.

Mai, who has worked as a model for various fashion brands and has gained support from women of her generation, is in charge of MARiA's artwork and lyrics. MARiA's sound producer, toku, has provided and produced music for numerous artists.

In March 2014, they made their major debut with "ambiguous", the 2nd OP theme of the TV series "Bounty Meteor", and won first place in the overall rankings of various music distribution sites, including the iTunes Music Store. Since their debut, they have released 10 singles, 2 distribution singles, and 5 albums.

In 2016, the dance video “Gokuraku” released on a video sharing site became an explosive hit mainly in Asia, and the total number of video views exceeded 60 million. A total of 10 dance video series titled “Dancing” have been played over 100 million times and are attracting attention all over the world.

MARiA's wide-ranging musicality and overwhelming performance power have become a hot topic.
